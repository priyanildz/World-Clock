About TimeWorld

TimeWorld is a global time viewer designed to help you track current time across different cities and countries in real time. Whether you're coordinating meetings across time zones, staying in touch with family abroad, or just curious about the time in another part of the world, TimeWorld gives you a live and responsive experience.

You can use the searchable dropdown to quickly find a country or region and view its current time alongside your own. Built with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, this tool is lightweight and privacy-friendly with no tracking or data storage.
